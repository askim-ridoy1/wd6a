About the Project

CREC4 Package WD6A of Dhaka Sanitation Improvement Project is located in the southeast of Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh. It includes E1A and E1B Zone, with a total construction area of approximately5.76 square kilometers. The contract value is approximately 200 million yuan, and the construction period is 2 years. The employer is the Dhaka Water Supply and Sewerage Authority, and the construction is constructed with joint loan funds from the World Bank and the Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank.
The project adopts a "design + construction" model and was jointly won by CREC4and Bangladesh local company ARIDOD in the name of a joint venture. The main work of the project includes:(1).E1A and E1B regional sewage pipe network design work;(2).Reconstruction / New Construction / Rehabilitation of Sewage collection Network in approximate length of 112.0 km with sufficient capacity to transfer indicated sewage flow in year 2050 using open-trench method;(3).Approximately 14,238 household sewage pipeline connection projects.
